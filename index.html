Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

How quickly can you start my project?

I can usually start new projects within 24-48 hours, depending on the complexity and current workload. Rush projects can often be accommodated same-day.

What's your revision policy?

All packages include 3 free revisions. Additional revisions are available at ₹200 each. I work closely with clients to ensure satisfaction before final delivery.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Yes! Express delivery (24 hours) is available for an additional 20% of the project cost. Super rush (6 hours) available for 50% additional cost.

What file formats do you provide?

I provide all standard formats including PNG, JPG, PDF, and source files (PSD, AI, Canva links) depending on the project requirements.

How do you handle payments?

I accept payment via UPI, bank transfer, and PayPal. 50% advance payment required to start the project, remaining 50% upon completion and approval.
